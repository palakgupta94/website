Top Destination Wedding Venues in Nathdwara

Sep 13, 2024

Nathdwara, a serene and spiritually rich town in Rajasthan, offers several beautiful venues for a destination wedding. Here’s a detailed look at the top wedding venues in Nathdwara, including their specialties and approximate pricing for 200 guests.

1. Radisson NathdwaraSpecialty:Modern Elegance: Radisson Nathdwara combines contemporary luxury with traditional charm. It is located near the famous Krishna Temple, offering easy access to spiritual and cultural sites.Versatile Spaces: The hotel features multiple event spaces, including a grand ballroom and lush gardens, providing flexibility for different wedding styles.Exceptional Service: Known for its high standards of service, the Radisson Nathdwara ensures a seamless and memorable wedding experience.Pricing for 200 Guests/function:Venue Rental: Approximately ₹2,50,000 - ₹4,00,000 depending on the season and specific requirements.Catering: ₹1,500 - ₹2,500 per person for a full-course meal, totaling ₹3,00,000 - ₹5,00,000.Decor: ₹1,00,000 - ₹2,00,000 for basic to elaborate decor.Total Estimated Cost: ₹6,50,000 - ₹11,00,000.

2. Shri Krishna PalaceSpecialty:Serene Ambiance: Shri Krishna Palace offers a tranquil and spiritual environment, blending traditional Rajasthani architecture with modern amenities.Intimate Setting: The palace provides a more intimate and personal experience, ideal for smaller and more spiritual weddings.Cultural Richness: The venue’s decor and architecture reflect the local cultural heritage, enhancing the authenticity of the wedding experience.Pricing for 200 Guests/function:Venue Rental: Approximately ₹1,50,000 - ₹2,50,000 depending on the season and specific arrangements.Catering: ₹1,200 - ₹2,000 per person for a full-course meal, totaling ₹2,40,000 - ₹4,00,000.Decor: ₹75,000 - ₹1,50,000 for basic to elaborate decor.Total Estimated Cost: ₹4,65,000 - ₹8,00,000.

3. Devigarh PalaceSpecialty:Historical Grandeur: Devigarh Palace, located just outside Nathdwara, offers a blend of historical architecture and modern luxury. Its expansive grounds and panoramic views create a majestic setting.Versatile Venues: The palace provides a variety of indoor and outdoor spaces, including gardens and courtyards, perfect for large and elaborate weddings.Luxury Experience: Known for its opulent decor and exceptional service, Devigarh Palace ensures a luxurious wedding experience.Pricing for 200 Guests/function:Venue Rental: Approximately ₹3,00,000 - ₹5,00,000 depending on the season and specific requirements.Catering: ₹1,800 - ₹2,500 per person for a full-course meal, totaling ₹3,60,000 - ₹5,00,000.Decor: ₹1,50,000 - ₹3,00,000 for basic to elaborate decor.Total Estimated Cost: ₹8,10,000 - ₹13,00,000.


Key ConsiderationsSeasonality: Prices can vary significantly depending on the time of year. Off-peak seasons might offer more competitive rates.Customization: Costs may increase with additional services such as premium decor, customized catering, and entertainment.Additional Costs: Consider additional expenses for transportation, accommodation for guests, and miscellaneous items not included in the base pricing.By choosing the right venue in Nathdwara, you can create a truly memorable and unique wedding experience, combining elegance with the spiritual charm of this beautiful town.
